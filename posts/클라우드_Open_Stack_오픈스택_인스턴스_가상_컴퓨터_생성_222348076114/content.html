vi /etc/httpd/conf.d/00-placement-api.conf

열어서 맨 밑에 다음 추가.

<Directory /usr/bin> <IfVersion >= 2.4> Require all granted </IfVersion> <IfVersion < 2.4> Order allow,deny Allow from all </IfVersion> </Directory>

그리고

systemctl restart httpd
